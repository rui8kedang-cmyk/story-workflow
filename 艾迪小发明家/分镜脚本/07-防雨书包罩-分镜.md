# ⑦ 防雨书包罩 - Seedance 2.0 分镜脚本

> 风格：迪士尼/皮克斯3D动画风格
> 总时长：约60秒（8个镜头）
> 角色：艾迪（小学生，头发乱糟糟，校服皱巴巴，表情丰富）

---

## 镜头1：难题（约5秒）

**画面描述**：
Pixar-style 3D animation. A messy-haired elementary school boy (Eddie) stands at the doorway of his home, pulling textbooks out of his backpack. The books are wrinkled and wavy from water damage, pages stuck together. Rain is pouring outside the window behind him. Eddie holds up a crumpled textbook with a frustrated expression, water drips from the book. Indoor warm lighting, rainy atmosphere outside.

**镜头**：中景，正面拍摄

---

## 镜头2：发明过程（约8秒）

**画面描述**：
Pixar-style 3D animation. The messy-haired boy (Eddie) sits at a cluttered workbench in a garage full of junk. He is assembling a device: attaching a waterproof rubber sheet to two telescoping tent poles, wiring a small spring-loaded ejector mechanism to a backpack zipper. Close-up of his hands connecting parts, sparks from a small soldering iron. His eyes are focused and excited. Scattered tools and discarded electronics surround him. Warm garage lighting.

**镜头**：中近景，轻微推进

---

## 镜头3：期盼下雨（约5秒）

**画面描述**：
Pixar-style 3D animation. The messy-haired boy (Eddie) sits at his school desk, chin resting on both hands, staring longingly out the classroom window at a bright sunny sky. He sighs with disappointment. Then the shot transitions: he looks out again, this time dark clouds are gathering and rain starts falling. His eyes widen and a huge grin spreads across his face. He pumps his fist excitedly.

**镜头**：中景，从侧面拍摄，缓慢推向窗户

---

## 镜头4：启动装置（约5秒）

**画面描述**：
Pixar-style 3D animation. Heavy rain pouring down outside a school entrance. The messy-haired boy (Eddie) stands under the school entrance canopy, wearing his backpack. He reaches behind and presses a button on the backpack with a confident smirk. Mechanical sound effect moment — two tent poles spring out from the sides of the backpack, a waterproof sheet unfolds and wraps tightly around the entire backpack in one smooth motion, perfectly sealed. Eddie looks over his shoulder at the protected backpack with a proud smile.

**镜头**：中景，从侧后方拍摄

---

## 镜头5：趾高气昂走进雨中（约8秒）

**画面描述**：
Pixar-style 3D animation. Heavy rainstorm on a school road. Other students are running frantically, holding bags over their heads, splashing through puddles in panic. In contrast, the messy-haired boy (Eddie) walks slowly and confidently through the downpour, chin up, chest out, arms swinging casually, completely unbothered. Rain pours down on him but his backpack is perfectly wrapped in the waterproof cover. Water streams down his face but he maintains a smug, proud expression. Cinematic slow-motion feel.

**镜头**：跟拍，低角度仰拍，缓慢横移

---

## 镜头6：打开书包——完美（约5秒）

**画面描述**：
Pixar-style 3D animation. Indoor scene, the messy-haired boy (Eddie) kneels on the floor at home, carefully removing the waterproof cover from his backpack. He unzips the backpack and pulls out textbooks — they are perfectly dry, crisp, and pristine, almost glowing. A magical sparkle effect around the clean books. Eddie holds up a textbook with both hands, admiring it with a satisfied, triumphant smile. Warm indoor lighting contrasts with the rain visible through the window.

**镜头**：中近景，俯拍书包内部，然后抬起拍艾迪表情

---

## 镜头7：镜头转向艾迪本人（约5秒）

**画面描述**：
Pixar-style 3D animation. Camera slowly pans up from the perfectly dry backpack and books on the floor to reveal the boy (Eddie) standing in front of a mirror. He is completely soaked — hair plastered flat on his forehead, school uniform dripping wet and clinging to his body, muddy water dripping from his pants, shoes making squishing sounds with water pooling around them. He looks at his perfect backpack on the floor, then looks at his drenched reflection in the mirror. His proud expression slowly turns to confused realization.

**镜头**：缓慢上摇，从书包拍到全身镜中的倒影

---

## 镜头8：结尾点睛（约5秒）

**画面描述**：
Pixar-style 3D animation. Close-up of the soaking wet messy-haired boy (Eddie) standing in front of the mirror. Water drips from his hair and chin. He looks at the camera with a sheepish, awkward smile and shrugs, as if to say something. Then he suddenly scrunches up his face and lets out a huge sneeze — "ACHOO!" — water droplets spray everywhere from his hair. His whole body shakes from the sneeze. The camera pulls back slightly to show the perfectly dry backpack sitting next to his soaking wet shoes. End scene.

**镜头**：特写→中景拉出
